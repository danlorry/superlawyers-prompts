## How to use this repository

Every prompt is ready to copy and paste directly into Claude. The CONTEXT-TASK-FORMAT-CONSTRAINTS structure appears throughout. Never skip any of the four sections.

Before using any prompt, read the relevant chapter. The prompts work best when you understand why they are structured the way they are — what each section does, what to verify, and where Claude's output requires your professional judgment.

## The Non-Negotiable Rule

> **Every legal citation generated by Claude must be verified against a primary source before it appears in any document you file, send to a client, or use as the basis for legal advice.**

This rule appears in Chapter 1 and governs every prompt in this library. No exceptions.

## Practice Area Prompts

The book covers general workflows. These folders cover specific practice areas in depth.

| Folder | Coverage |
|--------|----------|
| `practice-employment/` | Employment agreements, wrongful termination, handbooks, severance, discrimination charges |
| `practice-real-estate/` | Purchase agreements, title review, commercial leases, due diligence, easements |
| `practice-startups-venture/` | Term sheets, founders agreements, SAFEs, IP audits, equity compensation |
| `practice-intellectual-property/` | Trademark clearance, copyright infringement, IP licensing, trade secrets |
| `practice-management/` | Meeting summaries, engagement letters, status reports, BD copy, billing narratives |

More practice areas coming. See `contributed/` to add yours.
